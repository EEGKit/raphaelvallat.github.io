Welcome to my personal website!

I am a PhD student working at the Lyon Neuroscience Research Center under the direction of Dr. Perrine Ruby. My researches focus on the neural basis of dreams and dream recall using several neuroscientific methods such as resting-state fMRI, EEG/ERP and behavioral analysis. On this website you will find my academic resume, a list of publications and some tutorials regarding the analyses I specialize in.

  • Neuroscience Tools

Sleep

Sleep is an open-access Python graphical user interface dedicated to visualization and scoring of sleep data. Sleep is currently under development in collaboration with Etienne Combrisson and is integrated into his excellent Visbrain package for visualization of neuroscientific data. It is free and open-source so do not hesitate to download it on and checkout the documentation.
(see a screenshot of Sleep main interface)

SleepViz

SleepViz is the MATLAB prototype of Sleep. Contrary to this latter, it does not include hypnogram edition and real-time adjustement of display parameters. You can see a short tutorial of the different functions of SleepViz in the tutorial section.
