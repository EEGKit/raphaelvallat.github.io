Welcome to my personal website!

I am a PhD student working at the Lyon Neuroscience Research Center under the direction of Dr. Perrine Ruby. My researches focus on the neural basis of dreams and dream recall using several neuroscientific methods such as resting-state fMRI, EEG/ERP and behavioral analysis.

I am a PhD student working at the Lyon Neuroscience Research Center under the direction of Dr. Perrine Ruby.
